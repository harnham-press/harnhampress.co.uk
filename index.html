About

Harnham Press offer lithographic and digital printing services but we like to be a little different. Not only do we provide high quality print on time and and at excellent rates, we also like to work with you and provide a friendly, professional service. With years of experience, we are happy to provide advice and help you decide what is the best solution to your print requirements.

Our client base is diverse and includes a list of highly regarded solicitors and estate agents, although constantly growing we retain many of our original customers thanks to excellent quality control and customer relations. If you’re looking to source cost effective print then why not give us a call? Try Harnham Press - Every Time!

Services

Our lithographic printing facility offers excellent value for money on larger quantities but we also have a digital press which is far more cost effective on short runs. Capable of producing up to A4 6pp this state of the art press offers superb results and very quick turnaround.

As well as print we have a full in-house design and artwork studio. Our designers have a vast knowledge of the print process, which ensures that what ever we design, can be translated to a finished product. We’re always happy to help so give Harnham Press a call - Any time!
